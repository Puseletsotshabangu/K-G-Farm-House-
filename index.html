Our Story

Born from a passion for quality farming.

K & G's Farm House started with a simple belief: healthy chickens start with proper care. Established in June 2026, we are proudly serving the Kimberley community with quality live broiler chickens direct from our farm.

Every bird is raised on open pasture, fed natural grain, and given the space to grow strong and healthy.

  • ✓ No hormones or artificial supplements
  • ✓ Open-range, stress-free environment
  • ✓ Individual inspection before every sale
  • ✓ Locally owned and operated
